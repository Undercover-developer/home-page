About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas, working at the SCALA Lab (Smart Cities Applied Research Lab). My research focuses on multi-agent systems, distributed intelligence, and AI-driven smart infrastructure. I'm passionate about building scalable, data-driven systems that enable autonomous collaboration across intelligent agents.

Selected Projects

D-CAS: Distributed Content-Addressable Storage

Peer-to-peer distributed storage system implemented in Golang with TCP networking and SHA1-based integrity checks. Features automatic data replication, fault tolerance, and content-addressable architecture for efficient data retrieval.

Cloud Visibility Dashboard

Real-time analytics platform for cloud spend optimization and system health monitoring. Integrates with multiple cloud providers to provide unified visibility into infrastructure costs, performance metrics, and resource utilization.
